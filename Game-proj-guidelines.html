Turn-Based Battle Game

THE ZONE

Rules and Regulations:

    -The game starts by either the player or the enemy goes first. This is decided through a a coinflip.
    -Whoever wins the coinflip deals damage to the opposing side first whilst the side that recieves the damage cannot deal damage back for the meantime.
      -There are potions you can use while playing:
        Healing Potion: Adds 30+ health to your current health. Max health of 100. Can be Used 3x. Can be used both by the enemy and the player.
        Immunity Potion: Become immune to the next attack by the enemy. Can be used 2x. Can only be used by the player.
        Strength Potion: Next Attack deals 2x damage. Can only be used once. Can only be used by the player.
      -Damage dealt both by the player and the enemy ranges for 2 - 10.
      -The enemy has a 10% chance to deal critical damage to the player.
      -Attacks that are blocked only deal 2 - 3 damage.
      -If both parties decided to block, no damage will be inflicted on both sides.
      -The game ends when either the player's health or the enemy's health hit zero
